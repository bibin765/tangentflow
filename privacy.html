Privacy Policy

Data Collection

We collect email addresses when you sign up for an API key. No other personal data is collected. We do not use analytics trackers, fingerprinting, or any form of behavioral profiling.

Data Processing

PDFs are generated in-memory and returned immediately in the API response. We do not store, cache, or log document content. Your document schemas and generated files are never written to disk on our servers.

Data Storage

The only data we store is:

  • Your email address (used for account identification)
  • Your API key (hashed)
  • Usage counts (number of API requests per key)

No document data, document schemas, or generated PDF content is retained.

Server Location

All API servers are located in the EU, hosted on Hetzner Cloud in Germany. Your data does not leave the European Union.

Third Parties

We use Dodo Payments as our payment processor and merchant of record for billing. Beyond Dodo Payments, we do not share your data with any third parties. We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information.

Cookies

The TangentFlow website does not use cookies. We use localStorage to store your API key in your browser for convenience. No tracking cookies, no third-party cookies, no cookie banners.

Your Rights Under GDPR

As our servers are in the EU and we are committed to GDPR compliance, you have the following rights:

  • Right to access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — correct any inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Right to portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact privacy@tangentflow.com.

Data Retention

API keys are retained until you request account deletion. Usage data (request counts and timestamps) is retained for 12 months, after which it is automatically purged.

Updates to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. We will note the date of the most recent revision below. Continued use of TangentFlow after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
